    TRIAL TRAINING
    Want to try a free training session with us? No problem! Just visit us during our regular opening hours and speak with our team at the front desk. We’ll guide you through the process, help you schedule a suitable date, and give you a tour of the gym. Please note that due to high demand, same-day appointments are rarely available. For more details about your trial session, check out our Help Center.
    ABOUT US
    We established our gym in 2015, and since then, we have been proudly serving the Dhaka community. Initially founded as a dedicated MMA training facility, we have grown into a dynamic hub offering a range of martial arts, with a special focus on MMA, BJJ, and Muay Thai.
    Operating seven days a week, our team and passionate community provide over 140 classes, ensuring there is always something for everyone. Our spacious training areas, including both fitness and mat zones, offer the perfect environment for independent strength and technique training, as well as sparring sessions with fellow enthusiasts.
